Click the button for fizzbuzz!

In this exercise from eloquentjavascript.net, when you click the button below, all numbers between 1 and 100 will be printed, unless the number is divisible by 3, 5 or 15, where something different happens:
If the number is divisible by 3 but not 5, the output should be "Fizz" instead. If it is divisible by 5 but not 3, "Buzz" should be printed. If it is divisible by both, we should get "FizzBuzz".
